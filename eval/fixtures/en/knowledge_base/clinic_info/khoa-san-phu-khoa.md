# Obstetrics & Gynecology Department

The Obstetrics & Gynecology Department provides comprehensive reproductive health care services: caring for women during pregnancy and childbirth, and diagnosing/treating/preventing conditions affecting the female reproductive system.

## Services
- Initial emergency care in obstetrics and gynecology
- Prenatal check-ups and pregnancy management
- Common gynecological examinations
- Vaginal medication administration
- Cauterization treatment for cervical ectropion
- Colposcopy and cervical cell sampling for cancer screening
