# Internal Medicine Department

The Internal Medicine Department is one of the first departments to help identify health abnormalities. It examines and treats nearly every condition, such as cardiovascular, respiratory, digestive, endocrine, neurological, musculoskeletal, and dermatological conditions.

## Services
- Examination, treatment, and first aid for common internal medicine conditions
- Periodic general health check-ups
- Diagnostic support for conditions with symptoms of unclear cause
